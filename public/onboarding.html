Enable required permissions once to start automatic detection.

Quick Start

PSP Detector scans the current page for payment provider signals. When a PSP is detected, the extension icon updates. Click the icon to view provider details and matching evidence.

  1. Click Grant required permissions below.
  2. Open any checkout page and wait for the icon to update.
  3. Click the extension icon to inspect detected PSP details.

Tip: Pin PSP Detector from the extensions menu (puzzle icon) for one-click access in your toolbar.
