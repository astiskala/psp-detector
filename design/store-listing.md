## Detailed Description

_(16,000-character max — supports plain text only, no Markdown or HTML)_

```
PSP Detector identifies the Payment Service Provider (PSP), payment orchestrator, or transaction service provider powering any website — in one click.

Just visit a checkout page and click the extension icon. PSP Detector scans the page in real time and shows you the provider's logo, a plain-English description, and a direct link to their website.

────────────────────────────────────
KEY FEATURES
────────────────────────────────────

✦ Real-time detection — Instantly identifies PSPs on the active tab without needing to open DevTools or inspect the page source.

✦ 160+ providers covered — Includes the world's most widely used PSPs (Stripe, PayPal, Adyen, Checkout.com, Square, Braintree, Razorpay, and many more), payment orchestrators (Spreedly, Primer, Gr4vy, Juspay …), and third-party service providers (Amadeus, Cloudbeds, SiteMinder …).

✦ Detection evidence — Shows exactly how the provider was identified: which script domain, network request, or HTML pattern triggered the match — so you can verify it yourself.

✦ History dashboard — Browse a searchable, filterable log of every detection across all sites you've visited. Export your full history as a CSV for further analysis.

✦ Provider deep-links — Jump directly to any provider's official website with a single click.

✦ Suggest missing providers — Spotted a PSP that isn't detected yet? Submit it directly from the extension.

────────────────────────────────────
WHO IS THIS FOR?
────────────────────────────────────

• E-commerce managers & merchants — Benchmark competitors' payment stacks or validate your own integration at a glance.

• Payment & fintech professionals — Quickly survey which providers dominate a given market or vertical.

• Developers & QA engineers — Confirm the correct PSP SDK is loading on staging and production environments.

• Curious users — Ever wondered how a favourite shop takes payments? Now you can find out in one click.

────────────────────────────────────
HOW IT WORKS
────────────────────────────────────

1. Install PSP Detector.
2. Navigate to any website — ideally a checkout or payment page.
3. Click the PSP Detector icon in your toolbar.
4. See the provider name, logo, description, and website link instantly.

────────────────────────────────────
PRIVACY
────────────────────────────────────

PSP Detector does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or browsing history outside your own browser. Detection runs entirely on your device. No accounts, no sign-in, no tracking.

────────────────────────────────────
PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED
────────────────────────────────────

• activeTab — Reads the currently open tab to scan for payment provider scripts and network requests.
• scripting — Injects a lightweight detector script into the page for real-time scanning.
• storage — Saves your detection history and preferences locally in your browser.
• favicon — Displays the site's favicon alongside detection results in your history.

An optional webRequest permission can be granted for deeper network-level scanning; this is never required and can be revoked at any time from the extension's options page.

────────────────────────────────────
SUPPORTED PROVIDERS (sample)
────────────────────────────────────

PSPs: 2C2P · 2Checkout · Adyen · Airwallex · Alipay · Authorize.net · BlueSnap · Braintree · Cashfree · CCAvenue · Checkout.com · Cybersource · Datatrans · dLocal · EBANX · Fiserv · Flutterwave · Global Payments · Helcim · HiPay · Iyzico · Mastercard Gateway · MercadoPago · Midtrans · Mollie · Moneris · MultiSafepay · Network International · NMI · Nuvei · Openpay · Paddle · PagSeguro · PayPal · Paysafe · Paystack · PayU · Pine Labs · Razorpay · Redsys · Shift4 · Shopify Payments · Skrill · Square · Stripe · SumUp · Tyro · Worldline · Worldpay · Xendit · Xsolla · and 110+ more.

Orchestrators: ACI Worldwide · BridgerPay · Corefy · Gr4vy · IXOPAY · Juspay · PCI Proxy · Primer · ProcessOut · Recurly · Spreedly · VGS · Yuno · and more.

────────────────────────────────────

Have a PSP request, bug report, or feature idea? Open an issue on GitHub or use the "Suggest a PSP" link inside the extension.
```
